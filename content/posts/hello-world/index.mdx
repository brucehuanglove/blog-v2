## Why did i choose kaori.sh?

Because of Kaori Miyazono in Your Lie In April which is the best anime I've ever watched.

Kaori Miyazono is a pretty, free spirited violinist whose playing style reflects her personality. Kaori helps Kousei return to the music world, and show that it should be free and mold breaking unlike the structured and rigid style Kousei was used to.
